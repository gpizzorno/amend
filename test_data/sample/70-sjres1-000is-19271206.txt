“ ARTICLE —
“ Section 1. War for any purpose shall be illegal, and
neither the United States nor any State, Territory, associa-
tion, or person subject to its jurisdiction shall prepare for,
declare, engage in, or carry on war or other armed conflict,
expedition, invasion, or undertaking within or without the
United States, nor shall any funds be raised, appropriated,
or expended for such purpose.
“ Sec. 2. All provisions of the Constitution and of the
articles in addition thereto and amendment thereof which
are in conflict with or inconsistent with this article are
hereby rendered null and void and of no effect.
“Sec. 3. The Congress shall have power to enact
appropriate legislation to give effect to this article.”
